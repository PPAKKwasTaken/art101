Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem Solving

The Challenge

Make pseudocode outlines of an everyday task and a simple computer game. Then turn those outlines into comments in the code.

Problems

Most of the problems we faced were deciding what game to pseudocode. And, how to break that pseudocode down into tasks and subtasks

	Prepare stove and pan
	Get 2 tortillas
	Add ingredients to 1 side of each tortilla
		Add sauce
		Add cheese
		Add toppings
	Put tortillas together with sauce facing inwards
	Cook until cheese melts
		Flip periodically
		Occasionally check inside to see if cheese has melted.
